ESP32 CAR

Developed a PCB-based robotic car powered by ESP32. The project involved custom PCB design using EasyEDA, integration of motor drivers, and implementation of wireless control. Additionally, I designed both 2D and 3D mechanical parts, and fabricated the chassis using laser cutting to achieve a precise and durable structure

graduation project

A robot that follows the line and works in the factory. It contains gas, humidity, heat and fire sensors. It uploads the sensor readings to an application and gives an alert in the event of a danger by sending an email.

It is also equipped with a camera for monitoring and there is a distance sensor. If there are obstacles in front of the robot, it will stop.
