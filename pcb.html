ESP32 CAR

Designed and manufactured a custom PCB for a remote-controlled car using ESP32-S3. The system includes a remote unit (ESP32-S3 + control buttons) communicating wirelessly with the vehicle unit (ESP32-S3) which drives DC motors via a motor driver, has a 5V step-down, 3.3V regulator for MCU, and outputs for lights and a servo. Responsible for schematic, PCB layout, assembly and firmware
